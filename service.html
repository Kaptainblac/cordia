Services

Professional Insurance Services We Provide

Investment Service

The interests of institutional investors, such as corporations and SACCOs, as well as retail investors throughout the wealth spectrum, are our focus.

Health Insurance

This refers to benefits and/or coverage that either directly pays all or part of expenses incurred during medical care or provides reimbursement thereof.

General Risk Cover

This covers unintentional losses or damage to defined property due to any cause that isn't explicitly excluded from the coverage, such as theft, fire, riots, strikes, and unintentional damage.

Business Interuption Cover

A policy that covers lost income in the event that a direct physical loss or damage such as that which could result from a fire or a natural disaster and causes a business to close.

Domestic Cover

This plan covers unintentional damage or loss to residential buildings, as well as the goods and items within. The coverage may also include owners' or occupiers' liability.

Motor Insurance

When a motor vehicle is involved in an accident, burns down, is stolen and/or brings about liability, automobile insurance shields the insured from financial loss.

Employers' Liability Cover

This policy will cover any expenses or costs faced by the Insured with the Company's written consent in the event that an employee of the Insured files a lawsuit, as well as indemnify the Insured against common law liability for damages and the victim's costs and legal expenses.

Public Liability Cover

Public liability insurance shields an insured individual or group from lawsuits brought by members of the public (third parties) for matters like property damage or loss, death, or physical harm to third parties.

Group Personal Accident Cover

Employers or organizations may take this out for the benefit of their workforce or members. It provides compensation for accidental death, permanent disability, temporary income loss due to disability, and accident-related medical costs.

Goods on Transit Cover

Covers loss or damage to a variety of the insured goods while they are being transported by road, rail, or any inland waterway within the policy's defined area of coverage.

Work Injury Benefit Acts Cover

This cover protects the policyholder's employees from unintentional injury, disability, demise, or occupational illness while they are on the job and carrying out the insured's operation or any project the policyholder undertakes.

This is provided under employers' act of 2007.

Unit Trusts

This moderately risky plan invests mostly in long-term government securities, premium corporate bonds, and money market securities with the goal of giving unit holders a high level of return and long-term capital gain.

Pensions

This ia tax-effective method of saving money to fund your retirement and/or other future needs

Life Insurance

This policy covers your spouse and kids from the crippling financial damages that can occur from an unfortunate event impacting you.

In addition to helping with debt repayment, living expenses, and any final or medical costs, it offers financial security.

Marine Insuarance

Marine insurance protects against loss or damage that occurs when moving property, which may include ships, cargo, terminals, and other kinds of logistics, from point of origin to destination.

Education Policy

This is a particular kind of life insurance policy that serves as a savings strategy and provides a lump sum payment to protect your children when they turn 18 years and are ready to start tertiary education.
